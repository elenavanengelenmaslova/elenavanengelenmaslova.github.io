## Available Courses

### Foundations

Build a strong Kotlin foundation for your team — from first steps to confident, idiomatic code.

- **[Intensive Kotlin Basics Training](/training/kotlin-basics/)** — A two-day deep dive into Kotlin fundamentals. Your team leaves with hands-on experience and a solid base for production work.
- **[Bite-Size Kotlin Sessions](/training/bite-size-kotlin/)** — A three-month remote program that fits around your team's schedule. Developers build skills gradually with practical exercises each week.
- **[Kotlin Crash Course Training](/training/kotlin-crash-course/)** — One intensive week covering Kotlin end-to-end, from basics through coroutines and testing. Ideal when your team needs to get up to speed quickly.

### Cloud & Serverless

Take your team's Kotlin skills into the cloud with practical, deployment-ready training.

- **[Kotlin on AWS Lambda](/training/kotlin-aws-lambda/)** — Hands-on training for building and deploying serverless applications on AWS using Kotlin and AWS CDK.
- **[Kotlin on Azure Functions](/training/kotlin-azure-functions/)** — Practical training for building event-driven serverless applications on Azure with Kotlin.
